Recycling

Battery Disposal (Lead Acid)
Did you know that all the components of a battery are recyclable and reusable. New batteries are primarily made of previously recycled components.
Batteries consist of plastic, lead and sulfuric Acid. The plastic can be reused in manufacturing of new battery cases. The lead parts are taken through a smelting process where impurities are separated from the lead. Battery manufactures use this lead to make new lead plates or other battery parts.

Installations

Building roofs are the most common location for the installation of the solar panels for a solar power system. Most roofs usually have sufficient space and capacity to carry the extra weight of the panels. Maximum sunlight on the solar panels is key for any installation. Solar panels may also be installed on the ground if installation on the roof is not available or required.
